**Effective Date:** April 29, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 29, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to SwipeShot!

SwipeShot is an app for quickly cleaning up your iPhone photo gallery. We take your privacy seriously and want you to understand how our app works with your data.

**Key Point:** SwipeShot works entirely locally on your device. We **DO NOT collect**, **DO NOT store**, and **DO NOT transmit** your photos, videos, or any other data to our servers or third parties.

---

## What Data We Collect

### 1. Photo Library Access (Photos and Videos)

**What:** SwipeShot requests access to your photo library to read photos and videos.

**Why:** To show you your photos and videos for review and selection of which files to delete or save to favorites.

**How It's Stored:** All data remains **only on your device**. We do not upload, copy, or send your photos/videos anywhere.

**Permission:** You can revoke access at any time through iOS Settings → SwipeShot → Photos.

### 2. Media File Metadata

**What:** Creation date, file size, resolution, video duration.

**Why:** To group photos by month, identify large videos, and display file information.

**How It's Stored:** Locally on your device, not transmitted anywhere.

### 3. App Usage Progress

**What:** Which months you've reviewed, which files are in the deletion queue, which are in favorites.

**Why:** To save your progress between sessions and not lose selected files when closing the app.

**How It's Stored:** In a local JSON file on your device. Not synced to cloud, not sent to server.

---

## What We DO NOT Collect

SwipeShot **DOES NOT collect**:
- ❌ Your name, email, phone number
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device information (model, UDID)
- ❌ IP address
- ❌ App usage analytics
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Any other personal data

---

## How We Use Data

SwipeShot uses data **only for app functionality**:

1. **Display Gallery** — show your photos and videos
2. **Group by Month** — sort files by creation date
3. **Delete Files** — delete files you selected from photo library
4. **Favorites** — add selected files to system album "SwipeShot Favorites"
5. **Save Progress** — remember where you left off

**We DO NOT use data for:**
- ❌ Advertising
- ❌ Analytics
- ❌ Selling to third parties
- ❌ Machine learning
- ❌ Any other purposes

SwipeShot **DOES NOT share** your data with third parties.

We have no:
- ❌ Servers for data storage
- ❌ Analytics partners (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- ❌ Ad networks
- ❌ Cloud synchronization

All data remains **only on your iPhone**.

## How We Protect Data

### Local Storage
All data is stored locally on your device in the protected app sandbox. Other apps cannot access this data.

### Encryption
iOS automatically encrypts app data when the device is locked.

### Data Deletion
When you delete SwipeShot, all app data is automatically deleted by iOS.

---

## Your Rights

### Right to Access
All your data is on your device. You can view it at any time in the app.

### Right to Deletion
You can delete all SwipeShot data:
1. Delete the app from your device
2. iOS will automatically delete all app data

### Right to Revoke Permissions
You can revoke photo library access:
1. Open iOS Settings
2. Find SwipeShot
3. Tap "Photos"
4. Select "None" or "Selected Photos"
